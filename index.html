Biography

The Monkees were originally formed as part of a TV series about a rock group in 1965, which premiered on 12th September 1966.

Although the band was formed for TV which ran until 1968. They went on to have real life hits, and now with over 50 years experience of live performances world wide, are also available to perform at various different events.
