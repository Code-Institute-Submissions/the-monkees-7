The Monkees - Day Dream Beliver - Official Video


"Daydream Believer" is a song composed by John Stewart and recorded by The Monkees, with Davy Jones singing the lead. The single reached No. 1 on the U.S. Billboard Hot 100 chart in December 1967, remaining there for four weeks, and peaked at No. 5 in the UK Singles Chart.
